Main Characters


Takemichi Hanagaki

– The protagonist. A 26-year-old man who time-leaps to his middle school days to save Hinata and his friends. Courageous, determined, but physically weak.

Manjiro “Mikey” Sano

– The charismatic leader of the Tokyo Manji Gang (Toman). Small in stature but incredibly strong. Known for his legendary kick.

Ken “Draken” Ryuguji

– Toman’s vice president and Mikey’s best friend. Tall, honorable, and fiercely loyal. Often keeps Mikey grounded.

Hinata Tachibana

– Takemichi’s girlfriend. Her tragic death triggers Takemichi’s time-travel journey. Kind, caring, and strong-willed.

Naoto Tachibana

– Hinata’s younger brother. Becomes a police officer in the present and guides Takemichi through time travel.


---

Tokyo Manji Gang (Toman) Members

Keisuke Baji

– First-generation founding member of Toman. Fierce and loyal but has a troubled past.

Chifuyu Matsuno

– Baji’s close friend. Smart and supportive; helps Takemichi navigate gang politics.

Takashi Mitsuya

– Toman’s Second Division Captain. Calm, strategic, and responsible.

Hakkai Shiba

– Member of Toman. Loyal and protective of his friends.

Smiley & Angry

– Twin members known for their teamwork and mischievous personalities. ---

Other Important Characters / Rivals


Izana Kurokawa

– Leader of the Black Dragons. Powerful and manipulative; a major antagonist.

Senju Kawaragi

– Izana’s younger sister. Strong-willed and independent.

Kazutora Hanemiya

– Founding member of Toman with a dark past.

Shinichiro Sano

– Mikey’s older brother. Legendary fighter and influential figure.

Valhalla Members

– Rival gang members who challenge Toman during key arcs.

Tenjiku Members

– Another dangerous gang led by Shinichiro’s enemies in later arcs.
